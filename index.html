EVENTS COVER

HT Direct can support all your events first-aid needs

Whether it’s a multiple-sited large event over several days or a small local community event, we can help support you.

HT Direct first aiders are trained to resuscitate and stabilise patients with heart attacks or injuries until the emergency services arrive.

We have recently covered events such as:

  • Music concerts and festivals
  • Corporate events and parties
  • Equestrian Events
  • Carnivals
  • Firework events
  • Fun days
  • Fetes
  • Football and Rugby Matches
  • Sport days
  • Charity Events and Walks

Lives could be saved by having first aid cover for your event.

Cover can be provided for an hour, or up to several days if required. Get in touch for a quote.
